About the Festival

The Afro-dominican Roots Festival, known as "the party with a purpose", is an annual music festival which started in 2005 as a one-time event to celebrate the Afro-Dominican roots and embrace our ancestors origins.It is the largest event celebrating African-Dominican culture and music in the Dominican Republic.Locally referred to as the Afro-Dominican Fest, it has been held in San Cristobal,Barahona and Santo Domingo. It features the best afro-dominican culture artists simultaneously performing on a main stage.
